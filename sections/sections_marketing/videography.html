Move It!: My Motion Picture Repository

For the longest time, making and editing videos were my passion. I made quite a few, and I liked most of them. As I look back now, there are certainly a few that could be touched up. Maybe, eventually. Enjoy them still, though!

    Foundation of the Community

    March 3rd, 2021

    • If there is one thing I can say about being an intern at the Community Foundation of Northeast Iowa (CFNEIA), it is the freedom to do what I like. With every video project, I was given full creative control and the results were, dare I say, fantastic!

    Election Year (at NISG)

    February 1st, 2021

    • Work never stopped at NISG! As the Spring semester is when NISG elects the next administration. And what did it mean for me, the Director of Public Relations? Another video, of course!

    The Director II

    January 29th, 2021

    • The 2021 Spring semester came to UNI and we at NISG thought the students could enjoy a re-introduction. As the Director of Public Relations, I teamed up with the President of NISG as well as many other UNI students to create this wonderful “State of the Student Body” video. Honestly, this could be my best work yet!

Enjoy!

    Lost Graphics: COVID Edition

    April 21st, 2021

    • As the COVID-19 pandemic was raging on UNI campus, the NISG administration was looking for ways to encourage students to wear masks to reduce the rates of infection. I figured I would make graphics that would look intimidating so that people would be more inclined to think about other people’s health. This was never posted, but it was indeed a fun little project!

    Lost Graphics: 2020 U.S. Election - Voting 101

    July 19th, 2020

    • As the 2020 U.S. Presidential Election was looming on the horizon, I was tasked with making an infographic explaining how UNI students could vote from UNI campus. This graphic never saw the light of day and I never quite got the explanation why. Nonetheless, it was incredibly fun and educational, especially for an international student like me!
